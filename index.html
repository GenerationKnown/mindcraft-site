A virtual world that encourages kids to explore

The goal of Mindcraft is to create and develop an interactive multiplayer learning environment that will not only educate our users, but will also satisfy their entertainment needs.

By building on Khan Academy's knowledge map, we have created a 3D landscape that users can explore and enjoy
